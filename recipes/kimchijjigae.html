Kimchi Jiggae

Home

Kimchi jjigae (김치찌개), or kimchi stew, is hands down one of the best ways to enjoy some properly aged kimchi. It's such a household staple in Korea that many families will have it on the table at least once or twice a week, if not more. It's hearty, cozy, and bursting with bold flavors that instantly warm you up.

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Skinless Pork Belly
  • Mirin
  • Black Pepper
  • Gochugaru
  • Soy Sauce
  • Gochujang
  • Minced Garlic
  • Green Onion

Steps

  1. Marinate the pork belly.
  2. Cook the kimchi in a skillet until soft.
  3. Place all ingredients into pot except green onion.
  4. Add water.
  5. Bring pot to a boil over medium-high heat.
  6. Once boiling, lower the heat to medium and continue cooking until the meat is completely cooked through -- 10 to 15 minutes.
  7. Serve with a bowl of rice.
